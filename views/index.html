The League

The MMBFL was created in the fall of 2019 as the vision of James Wetherhold and Liam Crane.


Their goal was to bring amateur two-hand-touch football to the students in Mechanicsburg, Pennsylvania. Originally created to include only members of the marching band, the MMBFL quickly became a hit and the league began accepting players from all walks of life and backgrounds.


With Wetherhold as Commissioner, the inaugural 2019-2020 season had four teams and almost 40 players total.


Seeing the league’s success in the winter, Wetherhold and Crane decided to continue the league into the summer of 2020. The 2020 season currently has eight teams and includes about 50 players and counting.

The MMBFL is always accepting new players and officials.
